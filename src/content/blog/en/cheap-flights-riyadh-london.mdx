## Riyadh to London: The Numbers

The Riyadh–London (RUH–LHR) route is one of the busiest international corridors from Saudi Arabia. Multiple airlines compete daily, which works in your favour as a traveller.

**Direct flight time:** ~7 hours  
**Price range:** $375 – $1,200 return

## When Are Prices Lowest?

### Cheapest Months
**January–February** and **November** are the quietest periods with the lowest fares.

### Cheapest Days
Tuesday and Wednesday typically cost less than Friday and Sunday departures.

### How Far Ahead Should You Book?
- **Best window:** 6–8 weeks in advance for economy fares.
- **Last-minute deals:** Rare on this route — don't bank on them.
