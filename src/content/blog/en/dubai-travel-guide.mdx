## Why Dubai?

Dubai is unlike anywhere else — a desert city where your hotel room overlooks sand dunes, and a pristine beach is 20 minutes away. The blend of modernity and Arabian heritage makes it ideal for families, couples, and business travellers alike.

**Mid-range restaurant meal:** $15–$35  
**Burj Khalifa ticket:** ~$33

## Top Attractions

### For Families
- **Burj Khalifa** — The world's tallest building; an unmissable landmark.
- **Dubai Mall** — Largest shopping mall with an indoor theme park and aquarium.
- **Dubai Miracle Garden** — Extraordinary flower gardens open November to April.

### For Adventure
- **Dubai Desert Safari** — Dune bashing with a traditional desert dinner.
- **Wild Wadi Waterpark** — Thrilling slides with Burj Al Arab views.

### For Shopping & Dining
- **Gold Souk in Deira** — One of the world's most famous gold markets.
- **Dubai Marina Walk** — Upscale waterfront promenade with restaurants.

## Quick Tips

- **Visa:** GCC passport holders and most nationalities enter visa-free or on arrival.
- **Currency:** 1 AED ≈ $0.27 USD.
- **Transport:** Metro is safe and affordable; Uber and Careem widely available.
- **SIM card:** Visitor SIM from du or Etisalat for ~$14.
